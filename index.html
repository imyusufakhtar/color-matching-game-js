About the Game

Color Matching Game is a fun and challenging game where you match pairs of colored tiles. The objective is to find all matching pairs as quickly as possible.

How to Play

  • Click on a tile to reveal its color.
  • Click on another tile to reveal its color.
  • If the two tiles match, they will stay revealed.
  • If they do not match, they will be hidden again.
  • Continue until all pairs are found.

Benefits of Playing

  • Improves memory and concentration.
  • Enhances pattern recognition skills.
  • Provides a fun and engaging way to challenge yourself.
  • Helps with cognitive skills and mental agility.
